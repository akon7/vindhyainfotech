Privacy Policy

Vindhya Infotech respects your privacy. This page explains what limited information may be collected when you contact us through phone, WhatsApp, social media, or other service request channels, and how that information may be used for business communication and support.

Information We May Collect

  • Name, mobile number, and service-related communication details
  • Device issue descriptions, location details, or installation requirements shared by you
  • Basic message history needed to respond to your support request

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to service requests and customer support queries
  • To schedule repair visits, remote support, or CCTV and networking work
  • To follow up about ongoing service, maintenance, or quotations

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may only share limited details with technicians, support staff, or required service partners when necessary to complete the requested work.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact Vindhya Infotech directly through the phone or WhatsApp details shared on the website.
